Where to start?

As I have begun my journey into the vast reaches of web development, I keep asking myself where should I even start? If you ask your favorite search engine this question in relation to coding and web developement, you will likely find many different answers. None of which seem to be right, or wrong for that matter. The truth is it comes down to what YOU want to do, what interests you, and what projects you want to work on. With that being said, there are some common consenses that you may want to take into consideration. Welcome the three powerhouses of the current internet, HTML(Hypertext Markup Language), CSS (Cascading Style Sheets), and JS(JavaScript). If you do not have an answer to the aforementioned questions, these are a safe bet for a starting point. These languages are the current building blocks of the internet as we know it, and while there are alternatives, frameworks, and libraries that you may need to know, most is based off these languages or you knowing the fundamentals of these three.

-Tom Craig 4/30/2021
