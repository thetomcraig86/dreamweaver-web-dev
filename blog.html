Overwhelmed? You are not alone.

Web development, coding, information technology, application development, and other related topics, are a dynamic, complex, and always changing fields. Feeling overwhelmed when you begin is normal. Actually I would find it out of the ordinary if you didn't feel overwhelmed when you first got started. Would make me wonder if you grasp the vast complexity and possibilies these fields embody. So being overwhelmed is something you may likely have to get used to, so the real issue isn't being overwhelmed, it's how you handle it. My current advise to myself is to not focus on all that I do not know, but utilize what I do know and expand on it. Break the problem into managible tasks and don't forget to step back and look at the big picture.

-Tom Craig 4/27/2021
